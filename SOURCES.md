# Research Sources Used For Tone Rules

These sources were used to build the embedded offline settings and routing logic.

1. BOSS DS-1 product page (controls, behavior, tonal characteristics)
   - https://www.boss.info/us/products/ds-1/

2. BOSS SD-1 product page (controls, asymmetrical clipping behavior, stacking guidance)
   - https://www.boss.info/global/products/sd-1/

3. BOSS RC-30 product page/spec details (rhythm types, track controls, loop behavior)
   - https://www.boss.info/us/products/rc-30/

4. BOSS CS-3 product page (control set and compressor behavior)
   - https://www.boss.info/us/products/cs-3/

5. BOSS BD-2 product page (gain/tone/level controls and tonal behavior)
   - https://www.boss.info/us/products/bd-2/

6. BOSS GE-7 product page (7-band centers and +/-15 dB style usage context)
   - https://www.boss.info/global/products/ge-7/

7. BOSS CH-1 product page (E.Level / EQ / Rate / Depth control layout)
   - https://www.boss.info/us/products/ch-1/

8. BOSS DD-3T product page (DD-3 control layout and delay behavior)
   - https://www.boss.info/us/products/dd-3t/

9. BOSS signal-chain guide (pedal ordering concepts, looper placement note, EQ placement debate)
   - https://articles.boss.info/the-ultimate-guide-to-guitar-effects-pedal-order-and-signal-chain/

10. BOSS Pedal Partners: EQ article (practical frequency regions for pre/post gain EQ)
   - https://articles.boss.info/pedal-partners-combining-eq-with-other-effects/

11. MXR M108S manual (10-band frequency centers and +/-12 dB behavior)
   - https://www.jimdunlop.com/content/manuals/M108S.pdf

12. Orange Rockerverb 100 MKIII product/spec page (channel layout and EQ/power topology)
   - https://orangeamps.com/en-us/products/rockerverb-100-mkiii

13. Marshall JCM800 2203 product page (master volume architecture, inputs, EQ behavior)
   - https://www.marshall.com/amps/products/amps/vintage-reissues/jcm800-2203

14. Fender Acoustic 100 product page (acoustic control panel and channel controls)
   - https://www.fender.com/products/acoustic-100

15. Fender Twin Reverb product family pages (voicing and classic Twin control concepts)
   - https://www.fender.com/products/65-twin-reverb
   - https://www.fender.com/en-US/guitar-amplifiers/contemporary-digital/tone-master-twin-reverb/2274200000.html

16. VOX AC30C2 product page (channel architecture and classic AC30 voicing context)
   - https://voxamps.com/ja/product/ac30c2-cvr/

17. Mesa/Boogie Dual Rectifier product/spec pages (channel modes and control topology)
   - https://legacy.mesaboogie.com/amplifiers/electric/rectifier-series/dual-rectifier/index.html
   - https://legacy.mesaboogie.com/amplifiers/electric/rectifier-series/dual-rectifier/head.html
   - https://www.mesaboogie.com/en-US/Amp/2.DR.1.A.LC/2-DR-1-A-LC

18. Taylor Expression System 2 references (acoustic onboard control context: volume, bass, treble)
   - https://www.taylorguitars.com/support/electronics/expression-system-2
   - https://www.taylorguitars.com/support/electronics

19. Fender Telecaster control layout references (master volume, master tone, 3-position switching)
   - https://jp.fender.com/products/player-ii-telecaster-left-handed
   - https://www.fender.com/products/player-ii-telecaster

20. Gibson-style dual volume/tone with 3-way toggle references
   - https://www.gibson.com/products/gibson-les-paul-standard-60s-plain-top-ebony
   - https://www.gibson.com/products/epiphone-es-335-dark-walnut

## Note

Genre presets and rule tables in `data/config.js` are practical voicing heuristics inferred from the sources above and common pedalboard practice, then adapted to your exact pedal list.
