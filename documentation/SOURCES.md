## Policy
- Source-backed tuning changes must update `SOURCES.md` (root).
- Architecture/deployment decisions must be logged in `documentation/CHANGELOG.md` and cite primary docs.
- Opinion/community sources are allowed for heuristics but must be labeled as opinion and paired with at least one primary source.
